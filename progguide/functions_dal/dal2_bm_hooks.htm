DAL2 Business method hooks
Overview

For DAL2 DALs it is possible to let the 4GL engine perform automatic disabling/enabling and removal of specific form commands of type business method. In order to achieve this, you should program the following hooks for a business method:

  • business.method.is.allowed()
  • business.method.is.never.allowed()
