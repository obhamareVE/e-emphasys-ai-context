42I61 - REFERS TO predicate in WHERE clause of DELETE statement

An SQL DELETE statement contains a REFERS TO predicate in its WHERE clause.

Example
delete from dbtst120
where  workdept refers to dbtst100

The WHERE clause of a DELETE statement may not contain a REFERS TO predicate, unless it is part of the WHERE clause of a subquery of this DELETE statement. This statement will cause the error:

SQLState 42I61: REFERS TO predicate in WHERE clause of DELETE statement
