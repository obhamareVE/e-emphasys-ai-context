Form and form field operations overview

Use these functions to perform form and form field operations. For example:

  • to disable and enable commands and fields
  • to display particular fields
  • to update total fields
  • to execute a function for one or all occurrences on a form
  • to execute standard commands
  • to display default field values
  • to make fields visible or invisible
  • to mark and unmark occurrences
  • to display fill characters in fields
  • to refresh the current record
  • to reset a field to its previous value
